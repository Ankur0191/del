About Path Finders 2.0

About

Path-Finder 2.0 is not just an event—it’s a thriving ecosystem that nurtures innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship in the fields of science and technology. Designed to empower young innovators and startup enthusiasts, this platform provides a unique opportunity to turn ideas into reality by connecting with industry experts, investors, and like-minded visionaries.

What to Expect?

Pre-Incubation Workshops: Get hands-on training in ideation, market validation, and business development, equipping you with the skills to build a successful startup.

Panel Discussions: Hear from renowned tech founders, investors, and industry leaders, gaining valuable insights and inspiration.

Startup Pitch Competition: Pitch your ideas in front of Angel Investors and Venture Capitalists, with the potential to secure funding and mentorship.

Networking Sessions: Build meaningful connections with investors, mentors, and fellow entrepreneurs to explore collaborations and opportunities.

Bike Riders' Showcase: Kickstart the event with an adrenaline-filled stunt performance, adding energy and excitement to the experience.

War of DJs: As the event concludes on Day 2, enjoy an electrifying DJ battle featuring top performers, setting the stage for an unforgettable celebration.

At Path-Finder 2.0, we believe that great ideas deserve a platform to flourish. Whether you are a budding entrepreneur, a startup enthusiast, or an investor, this is your chance to be part of an exciting journey that shapes the future of innovation.
